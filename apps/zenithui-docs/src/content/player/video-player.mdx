# Video Player

A custom, sleek video player built with Framer Motion, offering modern playback controls, progress sweeping, and volume sliders.

## 🚀 Installation through Shadcn CLI

<NPMTab path={`${process.env.NEXT_PUBLIC_SITE_URL}/r/video-player.json`} />

Note: Make sure your Shadcn CLI is initialized in your project.
